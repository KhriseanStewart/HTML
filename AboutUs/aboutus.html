About Us


About Us

Welcome to Island Essence Café, where every meal tells a story! Founded by passionate food lovers dedicated to sharing the rich culinary heritage of Jamaica, we aim to bring the island’s vibrant flavors and traditions to Manchester, Mandeville. At Island Essence Café, we pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients to create our signature dishes and beverages. From the fiery spices of our Jerk Chicken to the refreshing sweetness of our homemade Sorrel Drink, every item on our menu is carefully crafted to deliver an authentic taste of Jamaica.


Our Mission

At Island Essence Cafe, we invite you to experience comfort in every cup, where every sip of our expertly brewed coffee transports you to a tropical paradise. Our cozy atmosphere is the perfect backdrop for enjoying our selection of artisanal pastries and fresh, local bites. Join us to savor the flavors of island-inspired culinary creations, and make every visit a delightful journey for your senses!


Our Vision

Our vision at Island Essence Café is to become the premier destination for authentic Jamaican cuisine and beverages in Manchester, Mandeville, and beyond. We aspire to be a cultural hub where individuals connect over shared culinary experiences, support local farmers, and promote the richness of Jamaican heritage. Through our commitment to excellence and sustainability, we aim to spread the warmth and vibrancy of Jamaica, one plate and one sip at a time.
